About me

Hi there!

I am a data scientist with a background in neuroscience currently working in health and eCommerce projects.

My expertise includes almost 5 years of python in data analysis, succinctly:
- exploring and wrangling data with tidy-pandas while taking advantage of vscode shortcuts (the more violent the keystrokes the better)
- Data visualization (bonus points for piping dataframes into seaborn facet grids)
- Warming the environment training models and processing jobs in parallel

I would describe myself as an analytical thinker, proactive, hands-on coder who likes challenges, specially those that lead to learning new approaches and techniques/tools. Finally, I have a soft spot for bots, GPUs, tinkering devices and long sentences.

My GitHub is riddled with (old) spaghetti code, however I am actively trying to improve my coding practices.

Thanks for reading!
